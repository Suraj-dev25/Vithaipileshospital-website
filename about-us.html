About Us

Overview

We are the best doctors and treatment providers for all types of anorectal diseases, including pile, fissure, and fistula treatment, for the last 8 years. We are serving patients with care and providing them with effective care without any recurrence. We offer advanced laser treatment for piles, fissures, and fistulas, and we also offer advanced painless surgery and kshara sutra treatment as per the disease pathology. All these treatments are done without hospitalization, and most of the patients are discharged on the same day of surgery. We have a well-equipped hospital, trained staff, special deluxe rooms, and an operation theatre. We have successfully treated complex, recurrent, complete, or partial horseshoe ﬁstula cases with an incontinence rate of 0% (follow-up time of no less than 5 years). These types of fistulas are handled by a special combination of methods. Our Vithai piles clinic is the best piles clinic in PCMC, Pune.

Globally, healthcare facilities are evolving to simplify treatment and maximize patient benefits. The goal of the research and clinical teams is to create anorectal illnesses such as piles, fissures, and fistulas that can be treated more effectively. Vithai Piles Clinic is the best piles hospital in PCMC, Pune, with similar objectives and a bright future. It has created the finest nodule treatment procedure for laser treatment for piles and the Kshar Sutra treatment for piles. Additionally, all of the modalities have been provided under one roof over the years by skilled hands.

Our Vision

To provide consistent high-quality surgical care with advanced medical technology in Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities. Creating awareness about anorectal disorders in society.

Our Mission

Improve the patient journey by offering comprehensive healthcare services and increasing patient-centricity across the healthcare system. To make people suffer less from piles, fissure and other anorectal disorders.

Our Values

Our team collaboration, our commitment to achieving targets and our commitment to integrity will enable us to achieve extraordinary goals.
