Treatments We Provide

Piles

Piles can be caused by straining during bowel movements, constipation, pregnancy or obesity. It may also be a symptom of other conditions such as diabetes and liver disease. Piles is a disease of the anus and rectum. Piles also known as haemorrhoids, mulvyadh (Bawaseer in Hindi) is a condition in which the veins in the lower rectum get swollen.

Fissure

A fissure is a cut or a tear in the skin. Anal fissures are tears in the lining of the lower rectum (anal canal) that causes pain during bowel movements. It is common for anal fissures to cause pain and bleeding when bowel movements are performed. Also, you may experience spasms in the anal sphincter, a ring of muscle at the end of your anus.

Fistula

A fistula is an abnormal connection between two body parts, such as between an organ and a blood vessel. The most common cause of fistulas is injury or surgery. Infection or inflammation can also cause a fistula to form. At Vithai Piles hospital, you can get treatment for anal fistula from specialized doctors with advanced laser treatments for Fistula.

Hernia

A hernia occurs when an organ or adipose tissue squeezes a weak point in the surrounding muscle or connective tissue called the fascia. The most common types of hernia are inguinal (inner inguinal), incision (by incision), femur (outer inguinal), umbilical (belly button), and hiatus (upper stomach).
